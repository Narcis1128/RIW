    Centric Express 2020

    de către Echipa Moodle AC- miercuri, 19 februarie 2020, 15:13

    CENTRIC Express is a program intended especially for students interested in improving their technical and soft skills.
    It is focused on raising awareness on the specifics of the IT environment in general and the specific disciplines, technologies and development methodologies used by Centric.

    The 2020 sessions are the following:

    S1 (7/03/20): Communication & Teamwork | AR/VR @Centric
    S2 (14/03/20): Object Oriented Programming (OOP)
    S3 (21/03/20): UI/UX | FrontEnd
    S4 (28/03/20): NET
    S5 (04/04/20): Quality Assurance
    S6 (11/04/20): Web APIs - GraphQL | CyberSecurity

    The tickets for every session will be available only on the week prior to the event in question. They are named after and are available only for that specific session.

    Example: 1st of March - 6th of March for Communication & Teamwork | AR/VR @Centric; 8th of March - 13th of March for OOP etc.

    You are eligible to enroll for Centric Express if you are a BA or MA student of the Faculty of Computer Science/ Faculty of Mathematics/ Faculty of Automatic Control and Computer Engineering/ Faculty of Electronics, Telecommunications & Information Technology/ FEAA – InfoEc.
